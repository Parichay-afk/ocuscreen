What We Screen

Three Domains.
One Session.

The only solution in the world combining ophthalmology, neurology, and neurodevelopmental screening in a single, school-based visit.

01

Vision Disorders

Conditions like amblyopia are harder to treat with age. We catch refractive errors, visual field defects, and misalignment early, when intervention makes the biggest difference.

Visual Acuity Color Blindness Visual Field Strabismus Amblyopia
02

Neurological Indicators

Pupillometry, Ocular Motility, and Visual Field data surface early indicators of neurological conditions including concussion, enabling timely referral where intervention windows matter.

Pupillometry Ocular Motility Concussion Detection Eye Movement
03

Neurodevelopmental Challenges

Dyslexia, dyscalculia, and dysgraphia do not improve on their own. Without intervention, children continue to struggle. We identify these challenges before they become entrenched barriers.

Dyslexia Dyscalculia Dysgraphia
How It Works

Smart Screening,
Effortlessly Delivered.

From setup to secure results, OcuScreen handles every step. Schools simply provide access. We bring everything else.

1

We Come to You

Our team arrives with portable, FDA-approved VR headsets. No specialist equipment or clinical staff required from the school. Plug-and-play, classes continue uninterrupted.

2

Children Are Screened

Each student completes a gamified session in 15 minutes, covering vision, attention, learning, and motor skills. Non-invasive, CE and FDA certified throughout.

3

Results Are Delivered

Our AI instantly analyses results. GDPR-compliant reports are shared with parents and school systems, with clear next steps. No diagnosis. No jargon.
